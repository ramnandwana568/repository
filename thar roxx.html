The Mahindra Thar Roxx is a 5-door lifestyle off-road SUV with a variety of features, including:


Design: The Thar Roxx has a new 6-pack grille, LED head and tail lights, and a metal roof with a large sunroof.


Interior: The Thar Roxx has a white interior, a new steering wheel, and 10.25-inch screens for the digital cluster and infotainment.


Safety: The Thar Roxx comes with 6 airbags as standard and Level 2 ADAS from the MX 3 variant.


Engine: The Thar Roxx has a choice of a 1997 cc petrol engine or a 2184 cc diesel engine. The diesel engine is only available with 4-wheel drive.


Transmission: The Thar Roxx is available with manual and automatic transmission.


Off-roading: The Thar Roxx has a CrawlSmart assist system, an electronically locking differential (ELD), and a brake lock differential for the front and rear axles. It also has three 4x4 drive options: 2H, 4H, and 4L.


Mileage: Depending on the variant and fuel type, the Thar Roxx has a mileage of 12.4 to 15.2 kmpl.


Space: The Thar Roxx is more spacious and upmarket than the Thar three-door, with a reasonably sized tall boot enclosure.
